Accessibility Statement

SwanTech is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We continually improve the user experience for everyone and apply relevant accessibility standards.

Standard: We aim for WCAG 2.1 Level AA conformance across core user journeys.

Assessment: We use a combination of automated testing, manual audits, and assistive technology checks during development.

Measures We Take

  • Semantic HTML, proper labels, and ARIA where appropriate.
  • Keyboard navigability and logical focus order.
  • Sufficient color contrast and scalable typography.
  • Responsive design that supports multiple device sizes.
  • Continuous monitoring and iterative fixes.

Compatibility

Our site is designed to work with modern browsers and assistive technologies. Some older browsers may not fully support accessibility features.

Limitations and Alternatives

Despite our best efforts, some content may not yet be fully accessible. We welcome your feedback and will prioritize remediation.
